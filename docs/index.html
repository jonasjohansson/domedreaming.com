DOME DREAMING
Stockholm, 7–9 May · Malmö, 12 May

Fulldome film festival at Wisdome Stockholm and Malmö, two newly built state-of-the-art domes. Join the open call. Enter and use our preview tool. Full programme released in March.

Dome Dreaming is a non-profit festival produced by Jonas Johansson, Fredrik Edström and Sebastian Häger, in collaboration with Aavistus, Kokong, Baltic Analog Lab and Nordic Audiovisual Artists. Student day in coordination with KKH, SKH, Konstfack and Östra Grevie Folkhögskola. Supported by Tekniska museet, Malmö Stad and Region Stockholm. 3D model by Ashley Reed. Original dome visualiser by Per-Olov Jernberg. Design and development by Jonas Johansson.

Dome Dreaming is not affiliated with Wisdome or Norrköping Visualiseringscenter.
